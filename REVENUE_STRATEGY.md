## Principles

1. **Free stays free** — published tutorials never go behind paywalls
2. **Sponsors, not ads** — no intrusive advertising on the site
3. **Quality content** — every paid product must be worth 10x its price
4. **Transparent monetization** — all sponsorships and affiliates clearly disclosed
5. **Community-driven** — sponsors influence topics, not editorial independence
